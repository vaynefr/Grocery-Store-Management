About Seven 11 Grocery Store

Welcome to Seven 11 Grocery Store, your friendly neighborhood grocery store located in the heart of Kochi. Established with the mission of providing the finest selection of local and international products, we strive to be the go-to destination for all your daily needs.

What We Offer

  • Fresh Produce: Locally sourced fruits and vegetables that guarantee freshness and quality.
  • Groceries & Essentials: A wide range of everyday essentials.
  • Dairy & Bakery: Freshly baked goods and dairy products sourced from trusted suppliers.
  • International Products: A selection of global brands and exotic ingredients for those who love to experiment with international cuisines.
  • Household Products: Cleaning supplies, personal care items, and more to keep your home running smoothly.
